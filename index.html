Wanna Know

How Al-Siha was created?

4 young family guys from Islamic University of Technology wanted to do something, as they saw firsthand that our market is plagued with rotten fruits- full of formalin. Thinking about food safety & family’s health- they promise to collectively source “preservation-free” mangoes & dates all over the country.
